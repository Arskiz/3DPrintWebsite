Welcome to Mercantec's 3D Archive! This is your go-to spot on the school network for storing personal and work-related 3D files safely and easily.



Whether you’re working on class projects or safeguarding your own creative works, our platform is designed specifically for you.



What We Offer:

Our archive provides a secure and user-friendly environment to store all your 3D files.



You can access your files from anywhere on the school network, making it convenient to retrieve your work whenever you need it.



Our Founders:

Developed by two exchange students from Finland right here at our school, Mercantec's 3D Archive was created to fill a need for reliable 3D file storage within our own community.



Seeing the challenge of managing numerous 3D projects, they built a solution that helps all students and staff manage their digital files with ease.



Local Community Focus:

As a service available exclusively on the school network, we’re focused on creating a supportive environment for our students and faculty.



We’re committed to maintaining a high standard of security and functionality, ensuring that your projects are safe and always accessible within our community.
